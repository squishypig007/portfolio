Key Behavioral Trends


Here is a summary of the key behavioral trends I was able to
identify through various projects

  • Consumers depend on Theory of Reasoned Action and Recency Effect: Placing interactive elements (like buttons/subscription links etc) after persuasive cathphrases makes the desired action more likely as the justification for the decision is recently available
  • Humans use cognitive schemas: Users tend to rely on schemas over active learning, so stick to the classics to ease navigation
  • Users rely on conditioning: Using a consistent/repetitive pattern eases parsing of information
